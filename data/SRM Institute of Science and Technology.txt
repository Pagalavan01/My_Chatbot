SRM Institute of Science and Technology (SRM IST), formerly known as SRM University, is a premier private deemed-to-be university in India, operating under Section 13(B) of the UGC Act, 1956. The institution is run by the SRM Institute of Science and Technology Trust and is guided by its Founder-Chancellor Dr. T. R. Paarivendhar, a former Member of Parliament who has contributed significantly to education in India for over five decades.

SRM IST is committed to pioneering innovation in research, transferring discoveries to society, and educating future leaders and entrepreneurs. The university motto is “Knowledge is Power,” reflecting its mission to empower students through continuous, relevant, and adaptive learning.

Governance at SRM IST is led by the Founder-Chancellor Dr. T. R. Paarivendhar. The Pro-Chancellor (Administration) is Dr. Ravi Pachamuthu, the Pro-Chancellor (Academics) is Dr. P. Sathyanarayanan, the Chairman for Ramapuram and Trichy campuses is Dr. R. Shivakumar, the Vice Chancellor is Dr. C. Muthamizhchelvan, and the Registrar is Dr. S. Ponnusamy.

SRM IST has a legacy of more than four decades in education and offers multidisciplinary programs across six faculties: Agricultural Sciences, Engineering and Technology, Law, Management, Medicine and Health Sciences, and Science and Humanities. The university operates multiple campuses located in Kattankulathur, Ramapuram, and Vadapalani in Chennai, as well as in Delhi-NCR Ghaziabad, Trichy, Acharapakkam, and Baburayanpettai.

Across all campuses, SRM IST offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in more than 100 disciplines, educating approximately 53,000 to 62,000 students from India and across the globe. The institution emphasizes holistic learning, blending academic excellence with industry exposure, sports, recreation, cultural diversity, and ethical values.

SRM IST is accredited by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) with the highest grade of A++ from June 2024 to June 2031. It is classified as a Category I University by the University Grants Commission. In national and global rankings, SRM IST has achieved 21st overall rank, 12th university rank, 13th rank in Engineering and Technology, 11th rank in Architecture, 11th rank in Pharmacy, 18th rank in Medical Sciences, and 24th rank in Research. It has been ranked 12th under the University category by NIRF 2024. Globally, SRM IST has been ranked among the top Indian universities by QS and Times Higher Education. It holds a 4-star QS rating with 5-star ratings in Teaching, Employability, Innovation, Online Learning, Inclusiveness, and Social Responsibility. SRM IST ranked 4th in India and 147th globally in the UI Green Metric World University Rankings on Sustainability.

The university is deeply committed to sustainability and green initiatives. SRM IST offers 176 courses focused on sustainability and green initiatives and has undertaken 108 funded projects worth Rs. 44.56 crores in areas such as climate change, energy, waste management, water conservation, transport, and healthcare. The university has implemented large-scale initiatives including solar panel installations mitigating over 12,609 tons of CO2, solar steam generators producing 1,500 kilograms of steam per day, installation of solar heaters mitigating 67,000 tons of CO2, biogas plants, replacement of CFL bulbs with LEDs, recycling 34 percent of total waste, cleaning oil spills along the Ennore coastline, deepening the Potheri Tangal Lake, and conducting Stop Plastic campaigns. As a result, SRM IST ranked 9th in India and 392nd globally in UI Green Metric Rankings and is within the top 50 institutions in Swachh Bharat Campus rankings.

The Student Counseling Centre (SCC) at SRM IST serves as a proactive support system for students, educators, and parents. It addresses academic stress, exam anxiety, emotional well-being, cultural adaptation, confidence issues, hostel life challenges, and decision-making concerns. Counseling sessions are conducted by trained faculty and experts in a confidential and supportive environment.

The Career Centre at SRM IST acts as the focal point for career development and placements. It facilitates campus recruitment, pre-placement training, internships, industry interaction, alumni engagement, and skill development in emerging technologies. The Career Centre supports recruitment by national and international companies and provides infrastructure for interviews, group discussions, and assessments. The Academic Interfacing Program (AIP) strengthens industry-academia collaboration by aligning curriculum with industry needs and providing certifications endorsed by industry partners.

SRM IST has an extensive research ecosystem. The university has published over 30,177 research papers indexed in Scopus with more than 240,000 citations and an H-index of 145. It has filed 833 patents, with 784 published, and hosts over 3,800 research scholars. SRM IST faculty members have been recognized among the top 2 percent of scientists globally by Stanford University. The institution has received major research grants from DST, DRDO, ISRO, ICMR, DBT, MNRE, CSIR, BRNS, and other agencies. SRM IST ranked 24th in NIRF 2024 for Research and ranked second among private universities in the Nature Index Ranking 2023.

Innovation and entrepreneurship are supported through facilities such as FabLab, a 6,500 square-foot makerspace equipped with advanced prototyping tools including 3D printers, laser cutters, CNC machines, and PCB fabrication units. The NewGen Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Cell (IEDC) has received grants worth Rs. 2.87 crores and supports startups through seed funding of up to Rs. 25 lakhs. Events such as hackathons, bootcamps, Triumph Talks, and expos foster entrepreneurial thinking.

SRM IST promotes global exposure through its Office of International Relations, facilitating student exchange programs, semester abroad opportunities, and collaborations with over 18 foreign universities. Programs such as the Indo-German Centre for Higher Education allow students to complete part of their education in Germany. Language training is mandatory for B.Tech students, with offerings in German, French, Japanese, Chinese, and Korean. The Taiwan Education Centre provides Mandarin language training and certification.

The Ramapuram campus of SRM IST is located at Bharathi Salai, Ramapuram, Chennai – 600089. It is well connected to Chennai International Airport (10 km), Chennai Central Railway Station (15 km), Chennai New Bus Terminus (30 km), and the nearest metro station (1 km). The campus features modern academic blocks, administrative buildings, hospitals, hostels, sports complexes, and green spaces.

SRM Ramapuram offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs including B.Tech in Artificial Intelligence, Computer Science and Engineering, CSE with specializations in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, Big Data Analytics, Cyber Security, Cloud Computing, Gaming Technology, Internet of Things, Computer Science and Business Systems, Information Technology, Electronics and Communication Engineering with Data Science, Electrical and Electronics Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering, Biomedical Engineering, Biotechnology, Architecture, Interior Design, and postgraduate programs such as M.Tech and M.Arch.

The School of Computer Science Engineering, established in 2004, provides world-class infrastructure and industry-aligned education. Students have received placement offers up to 39 lakhs per annum. The Department of Information Technology achieved 100 percent placements for the 2020–2024 batch with over 200 offers including international placements. The Department of Biomedical Engineering recorded 100 percent placements with packages up to 10 lakhs per annum. The Department of Mechanical Engineering achieved placements up to 30 lakhs per annum. The Department of Civil Engineering offers strong industry exposure through collaborations with L&T EduTech, UltraTech Cement, and other organizations.

SRM Ramapuram provides three hostels each for boys and girls with modern amenities, including dining halls, study halls, libraries, department stores, internet access, gymnasiums, yoga halls, and recreational facilities. Hot water is available round the clock using eco-friendly heat pumps and solar heaters.

Campus life at SRM Ramapuram includes technical clubs, cultural clubs, photography and videography clubs such as CamOgenics, and the National Service Scheme (NSS). These clubs promote innovation, creativity, leadership, social responsibility, and community engagement through workshops, competitions, outreach programs, and service initiatives.

SRM IST Ramapuram stands as a center of academic excellence, research, innovation, sustainability, and holistic student development, preparing graduates for global careers and societal contribution.
