 Research-as-a-Service (RaaS)

What is RaaS?

XTROP's flagship offering that enables companies to outsource research problems to a dedicated team of researchers and interns. 

We handle everything from problem formulation to research publication or prototype delivery, allowing your team to focus on business priorities.

Engagement Models

| Model | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Sponsored Research| You define the problem. We research, build, and deliver. |
| Problem-to-Publication** | Our team takes your real-world challenge to a Scopus/SCI publication stage. |
| Challenge-Based Research | Assign a time-bound industry challenge. We curate the best researchers to solve it. |
| Proof-of-Concept (PoC) | We build and test rapid PoC for technical/business validation. |
| Co-Development | Joint development model with shared responsibilities and outcomes. |
